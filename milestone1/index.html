Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

This site will include summaries of the most famous Call of Duty games, images, history, and why each game was popular.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Information will come from competitor websites, gaming news articles, and official Call of Duty sources. Images will be from free-to-use game screenshots and official promotional material.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Call of Duty is one of the most popular gaming franchises ever, and many players want to learn more about its top games and history.

Why should a site be built for this?

This site will give fans one place to see the best Call of Duty games with clear summaries and comparisons.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Users should explore the games, read about their features, and learn which titles stand out as the most iconic.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal is to inform fans about the most famous Call of Duty games and provide a better resource than competitor sites.
